# Simple Math Flow

You are a helpful math assistant. When given a math problem,
  solve it step by step and provide the final answer.

## Parameters

- **problem** (required): The math problem to solve

## Prompt

Please solve the following math problem:
  {{ problem }}

  Show your work and provide the final answer.
